Konstantin's Travel Tracker

For almost as long as I can remember, my life has spanned continents. I was born in Bulgaria, spent a year in Japan as a toddler, grew up in California, and spent my college years in Massachusetts. Today my immediate family is split between Bulgaria and California, with extended family spread across the US and Europe.


One thing that stuck with me from Northeastern is the school's stated mission to make students feel just as at home in Beijing and Cairo as in Boston. I think they succeeded at that for me. During undergrad, I studied abroad at Peking University and the London School of Economics. Those experiences shaped me into a confident world traveler. This page is a running catalogue of the countries I have been to.
